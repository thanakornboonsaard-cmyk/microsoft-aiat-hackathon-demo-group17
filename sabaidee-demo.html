Is Thai massage safe for me? I have lower back tension and sometimes get migraines.
🌿
Sabaidee AI · Source-Backed Answer
Great question! Thai massage is generally excellent for lower back tension. Here's what the Oasis Spa policy document says:

For lower back tension: Traditional Nuad Thai with medium pressure on the lumbar region is recommended. The therapist will use a combination of passive stretching and acupressure along the sen lines.

For migraines: Please inform your therapist before the session. They will avoid deep neck pressure and instead focus on scalp massage and foot reflexology, which can help reduce migraine triggers.
✓ Safe — General wellness, AI-guided
📄 Source: Oasis Spa Chiang Mai — Therapist Protocol Document v2.3 (2024), Section 4.2: Client Health Conditions
